Pork Sinigang Recipe

Description

Sinigang is a native Filipino dish that is a mixture of tender meat, vegetables, and savory fish sauce. This sour soup is a staple dish to every Filipino household.

Ingredients:

  • Pork Belly
  • Spinach
  • Fish Sauce
  • Tomato
  • String Beans
  • Chili
  • Taro
  • Cooking Oil
  • Water
  • Sinigang Mix
