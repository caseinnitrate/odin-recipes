Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a meal consisting of creamy layers of pasta, meat and cheese. Making lasagna can take a lot of time but the results are worth the wait.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion and garlic, chopped
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • All purpose flour

Procedure:

  1. Cook ground beef over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Mix Spaghetti sauce into meat mixture.
  3. Spread meat sauce onto the bottom of baking dish and top with lasagna noodles.
  4. Spread cheese over the noodles.
  5. Layer meat sauce, noodles and cheese again.
  6. Pour the remaining meat sauce and sprinkle with remaining cheese on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  8. Remove foil. Bake for until the noodles are cooked and cheese is golden brown.
  9. Serve and enjoy.
