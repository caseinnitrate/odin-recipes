Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Description

Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a creamy pasta dish that you can make in less than 30 minutes. This pasta dish is perfect for all occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • All purpose cream
  • Butter
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta based on package instruction.
  2. Chop chicken breast into cubes and season with salt and pepper.
  3. In a pan, add olive oil and melt the butter.
  4. Add chicken breast into the pan.
  5. Pour all purpose cream, still and let it boil.
  6. Put Parmesan cheese and cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Add the fettuccine pasta and parsley.
  8. Serve and enjoy.
